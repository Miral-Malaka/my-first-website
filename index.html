About ITI

The Independent Thought Institute for Strategic Studies (ITI) is a non-partisan research center dedicated to producing rigorous, independent analysis on regional and global strategic issues. Founded on the principle that informed policy leads to better outcomes, ITI brings together scholars, analysts, and practitioners to examine the complex forces shaping our world.

Our Focus Areas

Security Studies

Regional and international security, conflict analysis, and peace-building.

Economic Policy

Trade, development, digital economy, and sustainable growth frameworks.

Governance

Democratic institutions, civil society, and political participation.

Geopolitics

Multilateral relations, foreign policy, and strategic competition.
